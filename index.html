Tap any team to send them through — matchups and scores update down the bracket; tap again to undo, or Reset. Who advances is decided by the market: the live per-game ESPN/DraftKings line for games that have one (that’s the win-% bar — hover for the source; auto-updates, including in-play), and the tournament-winner (outright) odds for future rounds that aren’t set yet. Your picks override any game; real final results lock in. The predicted scoreline always matches that winner — the margin scales with the odds, the goals come from each team’s form (opponent-adjusted); a close, even tie may go to penalties (the favourite wins the shootout). Future rounds and any matchup you create by picking show just the score, no odds bar.

Data sources. Fixtures, live scores & final results: ESPN scoreboard feed (site.api.espn.com, polled live). Per-game win-%: DraftKings 3-way moneyline via that ESPN feed, de-vigged (used pre-match & in-play). Future-round winners / overall favourites: team strengths fitted continuously to the live per-game lines (re-fits after every game, no key needed), anchored to a de-vigged consensus of outright “to win the cup” odds (DraftKings, FanDuel, FOX, Opta) — that snapshot can also be refreshed live from the-odds-api if a key is set. Predicted scorelines: winning margin from those odds, goals from each team’s 2026 group-stage attack & defence (opponent-adjusted), eased toward their FIFA World Ranking.
